Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Lasagna pasta shape
  • Basil
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Hollindaise

Steps

  1. Preheat over to 500
  2. Grease pan with butter
  3. Place a layer of pasta sauce, cheese, and basil
  4. Place a layer of pasta
  5. Repeat steps 1 and 2, adding hollindaise when desired
  6. Once you have used all ingrediants, bake for 3 hours
