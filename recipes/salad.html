Ingredients

Steps

  1. Cut carrots, avocado, and dates
  2. Add 1 cup of each green as well as the nuts and cranberries
  3. Mix in cut carrots, avocado, and dates
  4. Top with nutritional yest, salt, and pepper
  5. Toss salad
  6. Add oil and balsamic to taste
