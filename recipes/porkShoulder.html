Ingredients

  • Pork Shoulder
  • Carrots
  • Potatoes
  • Peppercinies
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Red Wine

Steps

  1. Dry the meat and season all sides
  2. Dice Carrots and Potatoes
  3. Place a layer of carrots, potatoes, and peppercinies
  4. Place the Pork Shoulder
  5. Surrond the pork shoulder with the remaining ingredients
  6. Add enough wine so that the pork shoulder is fully submerged
  7. Cook for 8 hours on medium
